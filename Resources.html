Vision for the future

The Fall semester was spent doing all of the documents for the product and trying to find the right vision of what we want the the product to be before we get deep into coding in the Spring semester. The Spring semester is mostly taking our vision of the product and bringing it to life. The schedule is color coded based on user time. Blue is client user functionalities, grey is administrator user functionalities, amd finally red is for staff user functionalities. In the next semester, we hope to create a product that can be used by the client by the end of semester. Due to this goal, we will have multiple testing phases throughout the coding process.
